Why Club GreenSock?

There are three primary reasons for joining Club GreenSock:

  • To get the members-only bonus plugins which take your animation skills to the next level.
  • To get the special commercial license ("Business Green" level). Learn about licensing.
  • To support GreenSock's ongoing efforts to maintain and enhance the tools. It's a way of saying "thank you" and protecting against common frailties of open source.

FAQ

  1. I'm using Node/NPM/Webpack - how do I import the tools?
    We made a special guide just for you.
  2. How big are the files?
    TweenMax is around 35kb gzipped and minified. For the rest of the file sizes, you can go to the download overlay and click "customize".
  3. Why aren't there CDN URLs for Club GreenSock bonus files?
    Putting them on a CDN would remove one of the primary reasons for joining Club GreenSock, the very thing that enables ongoing support and development. Not a very good business move.
  4. I'm a member - how do I get (and load) Club GreenSock plugins?
    Just log into your GreenSock account. There's a "Downloads" section of your dashboard where you get the latest zip file containing uncompressed and minified versions of the plugins, along with a special set for NPM users that works great with bundlers. Take whatever you need for your particular setup.
  5. How do I get CustomEase?
    Sign up for a GreenSock account (free) and download it from your account dashboard.
  6. Where are Google's CDN links for AdWords and DoubleClick ads?
    GSAP is whitelisted on every major ad network (exempt from file size calculations as long as you use their CDN). Google's are listed here. For other ad networks, please contact them for their CDN URLs.
  7. What is "EasePack"?
    We bundled all the special (non-core) eases into an "EasePack" file for loading convenience.
  8. Where can I get help?
    We have some amazing community forums.
